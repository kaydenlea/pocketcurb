# Mission

PocketCurb exists to turn personal finance from homework into decision support. The product should minimize categorization work, handle reimbursements and shared spending correctly, and shift the user experience from retrospective cleanup to forward-looking decisions.

## Canonical Mission Statement

Give people short-term financial clarity without the shame, admin work, or cloud-dependent fragility of traditional budgeting tools.

## Mission Candidates to Preserve

1. Build the product that minimizes categorization work, handles reimbursements and shared spending correctly, and shifts the experience from retrospective cleanup to forward-looking decisions.
2. In the 2026 environment of inflation, subscription fatigue, and FIRE interest, move from passive observation toward proactive behavioral modification that is actionable.
3. Win by eliminating the admin work of personal finance and replacing cloud-first uncertainty with privacy-first resilience, a narrative-driven experience, a Safe-to-Spend number, and a Crisis Cushion report.

## What This Means in Practice

- short-term clarity first
- trust before deeper insights
- first useful answer fast, ideally in the first session
- context and guidance over raw data
- proactive suggestions, warnings, and recommendations over passive observation
- privacy-first from day one
- less user labor, less guilt, less cleanup
- build a daily or weekly habit loop around check-ins and pre-purchase decisions
- fast shipping with strong listening loops can beat larger incumbents
