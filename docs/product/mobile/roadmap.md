# Mobile Roadmap

## Phase 1: Trusted Clarity Hub

Ship Overview, Cash Flow, Bills and Recurring, Accounts, Transactions, Quick Add, onboarding that reaches first value quickly, low-friction capture and correction paths, proactive daily guidance, the first forward-looking cash-flow view, and core resilience such as safe degradation, explicit freshness states, and manual fallback.

## Phase 2: Events Become First-Class

Deepen event grouping, ambiguous-assignment workflows, event budgets, collaborator handling, split and reimbursement correctness, and Event Details so events become useful budgeting objects instead of lightweight tags.

## Phase 3: Shareable Artifacts and Maps

Add curated digital receipts, opt-in sharing flows, favorite spots by category, private spending maps, and weekly money stories with quick actions. Keep privacy and location controls explicit.

## Phase 4: Stronger Resilience and Deeper Guidance

Add stronger running-balance views, Crisis Cushion reporting, smarter alerts, cash shortfall prevention, and stronger resilience patterns such as improved offline capture or local fallback where they materially reduce trust loss.

## Phase 5: Longer-Term Optimization and Expansion

Connect daily behavior to longer-term goals such as FIRE, investments, strategic cash placement, configurable guidance profiles, and potentially broader network or recommendation layers only if they deepen the short-term clarity core without weakening trust.
