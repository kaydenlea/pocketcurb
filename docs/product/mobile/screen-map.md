# Mobile Screen Map

## Core Navigation

- `Home`: Safe-to-Spend, Daily Spending Meter, key alerts, and short-term guidance.
- `Today`: today’s transactions, edits, ambiguous items, and quick corrections.
- `Add or Simulate`: fast transaction entry, simulation, split, event tagging, and notes.
- `Calendar`: forward-looking week or month cash-flow view with bills, paydays, and risk markers.
- `Events`: event or trip budgets, auto-grouped transactions, and event-level insights.
- `Settings`: budgets, privacy controls, linked accounts, notifications, analytics, and account management.

## Supporting Flows

- onboarding and account creation
- reimbursement resolution
- shared visibility and privacy-toggle controls
- crisis cushion or cash stress explanation
- auth recovery and secure device session management
