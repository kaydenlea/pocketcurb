# Mobile Screen Map

## Recommended Primary Navigation

- `Overview`: Safe-to-Spend, budgets by category, recent transactions inbox, event timeline, and upcoming transactions.
- `Cash Flow`: net flow, inflow versus outflow, forward-looking pressure, and entry points into Bills and Recurring.
- `Events`: event list, trip budgets, active events, past events, and event-level share artifacts.
- `Transactions`: all transactions with chronological, category, event, and map views.
- `Accounts`: bank, card, asset, debt, and balance overview.

## Dedicated Pages and Flows

- `Bills and Recurring`: settled, due soon, overdue, and unresolved recurring spend.
- `Quick Add`: global composer for transaction, reimbursement, split, or event creation.
- `Event Details`: event-specific budgets, collaborators, top categories, favorite spots, map view, digital receipt generation, and sharing.
- `Weekly Story`: private recap of the user's week with quick actions.
- `Settings`: budgets, privacy controls, sharing defaults, linked accounts, notifications, analytics, and account management.
- `Notifications and Widgets`: glanceable reminders, caution states, and shortcut actions.

## Supporting Flows

- onboarding and account creation
- reimbursement resolution
- shared visibility and privacy controls
- digital receipt preview and share confirmation
- map pin review or redaction for sensitive places
- crisis cushion or cash stress explanation
- auth recovery and secure device session management
