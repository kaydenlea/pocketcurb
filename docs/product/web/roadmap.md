# Web Roadmap

The web lane exists to support trust, demand capture, future share surfaces, and later SEO or educational content without taking product-priority control away from the mobile lane.

## Phase 0: Foundation

- keep the web lane on a current official Next.js App Router plus Tailwind baseline
- maintain a premium landing page, waitlist page, privacy page, sitemap, robots, and typed metadata structure
- prepare the domain and content strategy for `gama.money`
- keep waitlist, share-surface, and analytics plumbing intentionally unlaunched until privacy and MVP timing are ready

## Phase 1: MVP Launch Surfaces

- turn the waitlist foundation into a live, disclosure-safe intake flow
- add stronger trust, FAQ, and launch-readiness content
- align launch messaging with event receipts, maps, and weekly stories only where those features are actually ready
- align launch messaging with the mobile MVP in [docs/product/mobile/roadmap.md](../mobile/roadmap.md)

## Phase 2: Share Surfaces and Trust Objects

- add web viewer surfaces for curated digital receipts, summaries, or shared event links once privacy controls and disclosures are ready
- support truthful previews of favorite spots or place summaries where the mobile product already supports them
- keep shared pages artifact-first and privacy-bounded

## Phase 3: Education and Problem Framing

- publish educational pages about decision-first budgeting, Safe-to-Spend, shared spending, event budgeting, and forward-looking cash flow
- add structured content models that support search discovery without bloating the marketing surface
- keep all claims tied to the actual product lane and verification evidence

## Phase 4: Comparison and Category SEO

- add comparison and alternative pages only after product truth and attribution measurement are mature
- keep comparison messaging calm, specific, and evidence-based
- avoid SEO pressure that would distort the mobile roadmap

## Phase 5: Growth Refinement

- add privacy-safe analytics, attribution, and experimentation only after disclosure, retention, and operational ownership are explicit
- iterate on conversion flows using real signal rather than referral gimmicks or unsupported claims
