# Landing Page Strategy

## Positioning

Lead with the idea that most budgeting apps mirror the mess back to users, while Gama helps decide what to do next and turns real spending into event memory, useful maps, and shareable receipts.

## Message Pillars

- Safe-to-Spend and Overview as a trusted daily answer
- forward-looking cash flow over retrospective cleanup
- event budgets and digital receipts for trips, dinners, and real-life moments
- map-aware place context and favorite spots
- shared spending and reimbursement correctness
- privacy-first trust and reduced admin work
- weekly money stories and narrative intelligence that explain what happened and what it means next

## Conversion Strategy

- clear hero value proposition
- product explanation with concrete scenarios such as trips, dinners, and shared events
- trust section covering privacy and security posture
- waitlist or early-access capture with a focused call to action
- future examples of curated receipts or stories once the product can support truthful previews
- optional social proof later only when it is real

## Tone

Calm, premium, minimal, and useful. Avoid fear-based or guilt-heavy marketing.
