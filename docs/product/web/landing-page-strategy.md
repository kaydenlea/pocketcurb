# Landing Page Strategy

## Positioning

Lead with the idea that most budgeting apps mirror the mess back to users, while PocketCurb helps decide what to do next.

## Message Pillars

- Safe-to-Spend as a trusted daily answer
- Daily Spending Meter as immediate behavior support
- forward-looking cash flow over retrospective cleanup
- shared spending and reimbursement correctness
- privacy-first trust and reduced admin work
- narrative and event intelligence that explains what happened and what it means next
- Crisis Cushion as a future resilience story once the product supports it concretely

## Conversion Strategy

- clear hero value proposition
- product explanation with concrete scenarios
- trust section covering privacy and security posture
- waitlist capture with a focused call to action
- optional social proof later only when it is real

## Tone

Calm, premium, minimal, and useful. Avoid fear-based or guilt-heavy marketing.
