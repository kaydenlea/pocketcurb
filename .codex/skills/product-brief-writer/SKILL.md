# Product Brief Writer

1. Read `AGENTS.md`, `docs/agent-workflows/00-operating-model.md`, `docs/agent-workflows/01-product-doc-lifecycle.md`, and `docs/templates/product-brief-template.md`.
2. Capture the user problem, why now, target user, strategic fit, and success signals.
3. Preserve Gama's decision-first thesis, Safe-to-Spend and Crisis Cushion framing, admin-work elimination, and privacy-first posture.
4. Record constraints and open decisions explicitly instead of hiding uncertainty.
5. Suggest `pnpm new:product-brief -- <slug>` when a reusable brief file should be created.
6. Hand off to PRD work only once the product brief is concrete enough to guide requirements.
